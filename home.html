BIOGRAPHY

Loyce Velma Amondi is a young woman who was born on 10th.July.2002 to the Late Caroline Atieno Tula.
She is the only child of her parents,her mother died two years later after her birth,since then she has been living with her grandparents who are small scale farmers.
Loyce has never got to know her dad because her mum died single .
she went to Kogweno Primary School from her early childhood to grade Eight where she sat for her K.C.P.E and passed with 306 Marks.
She then joined Nyandoche Ibere Girls Secondary school in Nyamira county.
Later Loyce was forced to transfer to a home nearby Day school(Kobala Mixed Secondary School) after the death of her grandfather since the grandmother would not afford boarding school fees anymore .She studied in the same school upto form four and scored a mean grade of C PLAIN in her K.C.S.E exams in 2019.Due to financial instability Loyce was not able to secure a place in the public institution within the country,but by God's mercy and help from a friend she managed to secure a scholarship based in Nairobi offering Information Technologyy course which she was always passionate about.
